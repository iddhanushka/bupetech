Why Bupe?

To reach the true potential of your company in an ever challenging and dynamic world.

Do you have the overall visibility across your business performance indicators to manage your strategy?

Align business model, KPIs with people to achieve strategic objectives.

BUPE business solution can be used to define business models and KPIs alongside with competencies accurately. This indeed keep everyone at every level of the organization to have a clear idea on their tasks and their responsibilities towards the organization. This can improve operational efficiency and awareness of overall business operation continuously.

Get access to Dashboard on KPIs with multiple perspectives.

When structures and KPIs are well defined with time bound tasks for every level, our dashboard can provide all the curated information in a graphical view to get the current performance of the oraganization. For an example, you can see individual performance of an employee in one perspective and how everyone’s performance is distributed in another perspective with the impact to the company or buisness unit’s performance.

Do you manage your business on multi-dimensional perspectives? How balanced is your approach?

BUPE tells you whether you have balanced approach in managing different perspectives.

It is important the KPIs to be SMART (Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant, and Time-Bound) as well as distributed amongst different perspectives (Financail, Customer, Internal business process, Learning and innovation etc) with a balanced approach. BUPE asseses your KPI distribution amongst different perspectives and provides the insights as to how to maintain the correct balance, so that they can be adjusted according to current strategy.

BUPE balanced scorecard provides your with score for each perspective at each level.

Knowing the score of each perspective is important to you to manage your starategy based on the KPIs distributed and the KPI performance. BUPE provides the opportunity to assertain perspective scores at Company level, Business unit levels as well as Employees levels.

Does your employees have required set of Skills and Competencies?

Are your employees adequately trained?

Managing competencies of employees are a key aspect of a business which impact directly on company performance. With BUPE each employee is mapped in a 9-box grid as per to their Performance against Potential with the ability of digging data deep down, managing is far more easier and systematic as well.

Are your employees tested in their Competency Proficiency at regular intervals to be inline with your strategy with adequate skills?

BUPE provides the opportunity to measure the adequacy of proficiencies of skills and competencies of your employees at any point making an informed judgement on areas which they need to be upskilled enabling you to achieve the strategy.

Performance appraisals, Rewards and bonus distribution, promotions all in one. Like a magic.

With BUPE solution, no rush in year end appraisals, appraising will become a work flow across the organization.

When BUPE is implemented, the system will enforce a workflow at each individual level to each task at their hand. Since each task is bound to their KPIs, the superior can continuously appraise on each task of the subbordinates. During final evaluation, nothing else to complete, BUPE will calculate the scores which you can use as a base for rewards.

You have got the budget for bonus, can you assure the right distribution? With BUPE, Yes you can.

When the appraisals are being completed, BUPE will have all KPI perspectives against the defined buisiness model. With these information ready the BUPE can distribute the rewards and bonuses accurately based on the results tranparent manner.
